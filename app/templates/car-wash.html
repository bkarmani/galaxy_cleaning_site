Car Wash Services

At Galaxy Cleaning Services, we provide top-notch car wash and detailing services to give your vehicle a thorough, professional clean. Using premium products and the latest methods, we meticulously clean both the exterior and interior of your car, ensuring a polished finish that protects your vehicle's surfaces. Whether you’re looking for a quick wash or a comprehensive detailing package, our skilled team is committed to delivering exceptional results that enhance the beauty and lifespan of your car.

Our specialized waxing and protective coatings shield your car’s paint and surfaces from UV rays, environmental pollutants, and everyday wear, keeping it looking like new for longer.

Frequently Asked Questions

At Galaxy Cleaning Services, we know you may have some questions about our cleaning services. To make things easier for you, we've put together a list of frequently asked questions. From our equipment and scheduling to customized services, you'll find all the answers you're looking for right here!

What is included in your car cleaning services?
Our car cleaning services range from basic exterior washes to full detailing, including interior vacuuming, dashboard cleaning, seat upholstery care, waxing, and protective coating for the paint.
How long does a typical car cleaning service take?
The duration depends on the type of service you choose. A basic wash can take around 30 minutes, while a full detailing service may take 2-3 hours.
Do you offer mobile car cleaning services?
Yes, we offer mobile car cleaning services where our team comes directly to your home or office for maximum convenience.
Are your cleaning products safe for my car’s paint and interior?
Absolutely! We use eco-friendly, non-abrasive products that are safe for your car’s paint, interior surfaces, and the environment.
