Frequently Asked Questions

At Galaxy Cleaning Services, we know you may have some questions about our cleaning services. To make things easier for you, we've put together a list of frequently asked questions. From our equipment and scheduling to customized services, you'll find all the answers you're looking for right here!

What is a custom cleaning service?
A custom cleaning service is tailored specifically to your business's needs. We create a personalized cleaning plan based on your requirements, including frequency, specific areas of focus, and any special cleaning tasks you may need.
Can I adjust the cleaning schedule as my needs change?
Yes! Our services are flexible. You can adjust the cleaning frequency or scope of work at any time to accommodate changes in your business operations.
Do you use eco-friendly cleaning products?
Absolutely. We offer eco-friendly and non-toxic cleaning solutions that are safe for both the environment and your workplace, ensuring a clean, green space.
Are your cleaning services available after business hours?
Yes, we provide after-hours cleaning to minimize disruption to your business. Our team can work around your schedule to ensure a clean facility without interfering with daily operations.
How do you determine the cost of custom cleaning services?
The cost is based on several factors, including the size of your space, the type of cleaning required, and the frequency of service. We offer a free consultation to assess your needs and provide a transparent, tailored quote.
Do you provide specialized cleaning services, such as carpet or window cleaning?
Yes, we offer specialized services like carpet cleaning, window washing, floor care, and more. We can include these in your custom cleaning plan or offer them as one-time services.
