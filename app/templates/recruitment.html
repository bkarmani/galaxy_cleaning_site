Frequently Asked Questions

At Galaxy Cleaning Services, we know you may have some questions about our cleaning services. To make things easier for you, we've put together a list of frequently asked questions. From our equipment and scheduling to customized services, you'll find all the answers you're looking for right here!

Can you help us recruit staff for short-term contracts or seasonal work?
Yes, we can provide both temporary and permanent staffing solutions. If your organization requires cleaning staff for short-term projects or seasonal demand, we can recruit qualified candidates to meet your specific timeline and needs.
Do you provide background checks for the recruited staff?
Yes, we conduct thorough background checks for all candidates as part of our recruitment process. This includes identity verification, reference checks, and any other required screenings based on your organization’s policies.
How do you match candidates to our specific needs?
We take the time to understand your organization's cleaning requirements, the skills and experience level you need, and the type of environment in which your staff will work. Using this information, we carefully screen and match candidates who are best suited for the job.
Do you recruit cleaning staff for small businesses or only large organizations?
While we specialize in providing cleaning staff for large organizations, we also recruit for small and medium-sized businesses. Whether you need one cleaner or an entire team, we can help you find the right personnel for your needs.
