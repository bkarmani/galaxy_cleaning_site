Frequently Asked Questions

At Galaxy Cleaning Services, we know you may have some questions about our cleaning services. To make things easier for you, we've put together a list of frequently asked questions. From our equipment and scheduling to customized services, you'll find all the answers you're looking for right here!

What is the difference between sanitization and disinfection?
Sanitization reduces the number of germs on surfaces to a safe level, while disinfection kills a higher percentage of germs, bacteria, and viruses. Disinfection is more comprehensive and targets harmful pathogens.
How often should sanitization and disinfection services be performed?
The frequency depends on the type of space and its usage. High-traffic areas or places prone to contamination, such as offices, healthcare facilities, or public spaces, should be disinfected regularly. Residential homes may require it less frequently unless there's a health concern.
Are the disinfectants you use safe for children and pets?
Yes, we use non-toxic, EPA-approved disinfectants that are safe for children, pets, and the environment while effectively eliminating harmful pathogens.
Do I need to leave the premises during disinfection?
In most cases, it’s recommended to vacate the area during disinfection to allow the disinfectants to work effectively and for safety. After the process, we ensure proper ventilation before you return.
