Frequently Asked Questions

At Galaxy Cleaning Services, we know you may have some questions about our cleaning services. To make things easier for you, we've put together a list of frequently asked questions. From our equipment and scheduling to customized services, you'll find all the answers you're looking for right here!

What types of surfaces do you clean with your specialized services?
We clean a wide variety of surfaces, including carpets, upholstery, windows (interior and exterior), tile and grout, hardwood, tile, vinyl floors, and exteriors like sidewalks and driveways.
How often should I schedule specialized cleaning for my carpets and upholstery?
For high-traffic areas, we recommend carpet and upholstery cleaning every 6 to 12 months. However, the frequency can vary depending on usage, pets, and exposure to stains or dirt.
Can you remove tough stains from tile grout and upholstery?
Absolutely! Our team specializes in removing tough stains from tile grout and upholstery using advanced techniques and products, leaving your surfaces looking like new.
Do I need to prepare anything before your team arrives?
It’s helpful to clear the areas to be cleaned of personal items, but our team will handle all the heavy lifting and moving of furniture, as needed, to ensure a thorough clean.
